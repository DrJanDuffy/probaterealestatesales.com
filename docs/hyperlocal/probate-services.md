- **Probate timeline:** 6-8 months
- **Clark County court costs:** $1,000
- **Trust administration:** $2,500, 3 months
- **Probate attorney:** $8,000-9,000
- **Certificate of incumbency:** $750
- **Small estate limit:** $100,000
